BMI Calculator

BMI is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women. Use the BMI calculator to find out your body mass index and see if you're underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

The BMI categories are:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25–29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or greater
